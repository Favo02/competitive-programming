## ICPC SWERC

> Southwestern Europe Regional Contest (**SWERC**) is a prestigious programming competition organized by the International Collegiate Programming Contest (**ICPC**).
>
> It brings together teams of three students from universities across _France, Israel, Italy, Portugal, Spain, Switzerland as well as from Andorra, Liechtenstein, Malta, Monaco, San Marino, and the Vatican City_ to solve **algorithmic problems** in a **5-hour contest**.
>
> The top teams qualifies for the ICPC Europe Championship and ICPC World Finals.
